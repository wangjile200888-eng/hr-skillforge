# Quality Standard

## Quality Gate Philosophy

HR Nuwa is a methodology distiller, not a legal due-diligence system. The gate should prevent fake authority, but should not block useful distillation when enough high-signal public material exists.

Use three quality levels:

| Level | Meaning | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Full Version | Strong source base, broad coverage | Stable methodology models |
| Usable Candidate | Enough high-signal sources, especially video/transcript and official sources | Usable Skill with confidence labels |
| Light Version | Sparse or weak evidence | Candidate hypotheses and research summary only |

## Full Version Thresholds

| Target Type | Total Sources | Video Sources | Official Sources | Recent 3 Years |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| International consulting company | 50 | 8 | 25 | 10 |
| HR professional consulting company | 40 | 5 | 20 | 8 |
| Chinese benchmark company | 40 | 5 | 15 | 8 |
| Niche company | 20 | 2 | 8 | 5 |

## Usable Candidate Thresholds

Use this level for historical brands, merged firms, niche consulting methods, or targets where public material is fragmented but high-signal.

| Target Type | Total Sources | Video Sources | Official Sources | Recent 3 Years |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| International consulting company | 20 | 5 | 10 | 5 |
| HR professional consulting company | 18 | 4 | 8 | 4 |
| Chinese benchmark company | 20 | 4 | 8 | 5 |
| Niche company | 12 | 2 | 5 | 3 |

Additional requirement for Usable Candidate:

- At least 3 A/B-level source cards.
- At least 1 core methodology or tool family is clearly evidenced.
- At least 1 video/transcript source contributes to a mental model.
- The final Skill must mark confidence level for each core model.

## Required Checks

- source count
- video/transcript count
- official source count
- A/B evidence count
- latest 3 years count
- seven research files exist
- source cards exist
- core methodology exists
- diagnostic questions exist
- tools/templates exist
- China localization exists
- anti-patterns exist
- no claim of internal/private methods

## Search Stop Criteria

Stop when:

1. 20 consecutive new sources add no new methodology, case, tool, or expression pattern.
2. Core topic coverage reaches roughly 80%.
3. Usable Candidate thresholds are met and user accepts candidate quality.
4. Low-quality targets are marked Light Version rather than forced into full Skill.
