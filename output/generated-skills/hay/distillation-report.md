## Distilled Methodology Layers

1. Job value and job evaluation
   - Evaluate the job, not the person.
   - Use Know-How, Problem Solving, and Accountability as the public Hay-style diagnostic language.
   - Use profile consistency to check whether the role shape makes sense.

2. Work and career architecture
   - Convert job value into job families, career streams, levels, roles, success profiles, and pay bands.
   - Separate management and professional tracks.

3. Pay and performance governance
   - Separate internal equity from market competitiveness.
   - Separate job size, person capability, performance result, and individual pay.

4. Leadership and climate
   - Treat leadership styles as climate-shaping behavior.
   - Diagnose whether performance issues are actually caused by unclear goals, weak feedback, low trust, poor empowerment, or inconsistent expectations.

5. Talent and success profiles
   - Link experiences, competencies, traits, and drivers to business-critical roles.
   - Use success profiles for hiring, promotion, succession, and development.
