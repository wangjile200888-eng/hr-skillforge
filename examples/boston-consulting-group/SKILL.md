## Distilled BCG-Style Method Principles / 蒸馏出的 BCG 风格方法原则

### 1. Advantage Starts With Capabilities / 竞争优势始于能力

**Evidence signal:** BCG people-strategy materials connect talent, skills, workforce planning, and people development to competitive advantage.  
**证据信号：** BCG 人才战略资料把人才、技能、人力规划与人员发展连接到竞争优势。

**Operating logic / 运作逻辑:** Business strategy creates a capability agenda; capability gaps define talent priorities; talent priorities determine build, buy, borrow, redeploy, or automate choices.  
**运作逻辑：** 业务战略先生成能力议题，能力缺口决定人才优先级，人才优先级再决定培养、招聘、外借、调配或自动化。

**Deliverable pattern / 交付物模式:** Strategy-to-capability map, critical-role heatmap, workforce gap table, capability-building roadmap.  
**交付物模式：** 战略到能力地图、关键岗位热力图、人力缺口表、能力建设路线图。

### 2. Performance Depends On Context, Not Only Structure / 绩效取决于组织情境，而不只是结构

**Evidence signal:** BCG organization-design materials emphasize that performance changes require changing the organizational context in which people work, including behaviors, interactions, decisions, processes, systems, and structure.  
**证据信号：** BCG 组织设计资料强调，改变绩效需要改变员工工作的组织情境，包括行为、互动、决策、流程、系统和结构。

**Operating logic / 运作逻辑:** If behavior is constrained by decision rights, interfaces, incentives, or routines, changing boxes on an org chart will not solve the problem.  
**运作逻辑：** 如果行为被决策权、接口、激励或工作惯例限制，只调整组织架构图无法解决问题。

**Deliverable pattern / 交付物模式:** Organization-context diagnosis, decision-rights redesign, collaboration-interface map, behavior-change plan.  
**交付物模式：** 组织情境诊断、决策权重设、协作接口图、行为改变计划。

### 3. Distributed And Agile Models Must Be Designed, Not Announced / 分布式与敏捷模式必须被设计，而不是被宣布

**Evidence signal:** BCG distributed-organization materials discuss platform organization, agile stance, multidisciplinary teams, functional experts, shared services, integrations, and spin-offs as design responses to changing priorities and work models.  
**证据信号：** BCG 分布式组织资料将平台组织、敏捷姿态、多学科团队、职能专家、共享服务、整合与拆分视为应对优先级和工作模式变化的设计选择。

**Operating logic / 运作逻辑:** Agile or distributed design needs clear missions, team boundaries, expert allocation rules, governance, escalation paths, and shared-service interfaces.  
**运作逻辑：** 敏捷或分布式设计需要明确任务、团队边界、专家配置规则、治理机制、升级路径和共享服务接口。

**Deliverable pattern / 交付物模式:** Agile operating-model blueprint, platform capability map, squad/team charter, governance and escalation matrix.  
**交付物模式：** 敏捷运营模式蓝图、平台能力地图、小队/团队章程、治理与升级矩阵。

### 4. Leadership Development Must Happen In The Work / 领导力发展必须嵌入真实工作

**Evidence signal:** BCG leadership-development materials criticize isolated training and connect leadership development to transformational needs and real business context.  
**证据信号：** BCG 领导力发展资料批评脱离场景的一次性培训，并把领导力发展与转型需求和真实业务情境相连接。

**Operating logic / 运作逻辑:** Leaders change when development is tied to business challenges, role expectations, feedback loops, peer routines, and transformation milestones.  
**运作逻辑：** 领导者的改变来自业务挑战、角色期望、反馈循环、同伴机制和转型里程碑的共同约束，而不是单次课程。

**Deliverable pattern / 交付物模式:** Leadership requirement model, leader behavior scorecard, action-learning journey, leadership cadence and coaching plan.  
**交付物模式：** 领导力要求模型、领导行为计分卡、行动学习旅程、领导力节奏与教练计划。

### 5. AI And Digital HR Should Redesign Work, Not Only Add Tools / AI 与数字化 HR 应重塑工作，而不是只加工具

**Evidence signal:** BCG digital HR, BCG X, AI, and webinar materials repeatedly connect digital/human capacity, AI, GenAI, and transformation advantage.  
**证据信号：** BCG 数字化 HR、BCG X、AI 与网络研讨会资料反复连接数字能力、人类能力、AI/生成式 AI 与转型优势。

**Operating logic / 运作逻辑:** Digital HR creates value when work, skills, roles, data flows, employee experience, and HR services are redesigned together.  
**运作逻辑：** 数字化 HR 只有在工作、技能、角色、数据流、员工体验与 HR 服务被共同重设时才创造价值。

**Deliverable pattern / 交付物模式:** AI work-redesign matrix, digital HR roadmap, people-data model, HR service journey, adoption dashboard.  
**交付物模式：** AI 工作再设计矩阵、数字化 HR 路线图、人力数据模型、HR 服务旅程、采纳仪表盘。

## Diagnostic Lenses / 诊断镜头

When answering user questions, diagnose through five lenses:

1. **Advantage lens / 优势镜头:** What business advantage must HR or organization capability enable?
2. **Capability lens / 能力镜头:** Which capabilities, roles, skills, and leadership behaviors are scarce or misallocated?
3. **Context lens / 情境镜头:** Which structures, decision rights, interfaces, incentives, systems, or routines shape behavior?
4. **Operating-model lens / 运营模式镜头:** Is work best organized by function, platform, product, geography, agile team, shared service, or hybrid model?
5. **Transformation lens / 转型镜头:** What must be adopted, by whom, in what rhythm, and how will value be tracked?

## Anti-Patterns / 反模式

- Producing generic HR templates without linking them to BCG-style method logic.
- Treating org design as only reporting lines and spans/layers.
- Recommending leadership classes without work-embedded behavior change.
- Treating AI in HR as software procurement rather than work redesign.
- Calling a model "BCG official" when it is public-evidence synthesis.
- Hiding evidence gaps, especially missing video transcripts.

- 生成与 BCG 风格方法逻辑无关的通用 HR 模板。
- 把组织设计简化成汇报关系和管理跨度。
- 只推荐领导力课程，不嵌入真实工作行为改变。
- 把 AI HR 当作软件采购，而不是工作再设计。
- 把公开资料综合判断说成 “BCG 官方方法”。
- 隐藏证据缺口，尤其是缺失视频字幕。
