## Core Distillation / 核心蒸馏

The final Skill distills Deloitte Human Capital into seven method families.  
最终 Skill 将 Deloitte Human Capital 蒸馏为七类方法族。

1. Human performance and human outcomes / 人类绩效与人的结果
2. Boundaryless HR / 无边界 HR
3. High-Impact HR Operating Model / 高影响力 HR 运营模式
4. Organization design and networked teams / 组织设计与网络化团队
5. Performance management reinvention / 绩效管理重塑
6. Total rewards and workforce experience / 全面回报与劳动力体验
7. Human-machine workforce and AI / 人机协作与 AI

## Best Use / 最适合用途

- Diagnose HR and workforce issues through both business outcomes and human outcomes.  
  同时用业务结果和人的结果诊断 HR 与劳动力问题。
- Redesign HR from a siloed function into a boundaryless discipline shared with the business.  
  将 HR 从孤立职能重设计为与业务共同承担的无边界纪律。
- Build High-Impact HR operating models around customers, capabilities, digital workflows, data, and decisions.  
  围绕客户、能力、数字化流程、数据和决策构建高影响力 HR 运营模式。
- Translate strategy into organization design, roles, networks of teams, KPIs, and governance.  
  将战略转化为组织设计、角色、团队网络、KPI 和治理。
