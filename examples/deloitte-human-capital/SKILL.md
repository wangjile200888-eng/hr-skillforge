## Core Deloitte Human Capital Logic / Deloitte Human Capital 核心逻辑

Use this logic before designing any solution.  
设计任何方案前，先使用以下逻辑。

```text
1. Human performance equation / 人类绩效方程
   What business outcomes and human outcomes must improve together?
   哪些业务结果和人的结果必须共同改善？

2. Work and workforce boundary / 工作与劳动力边界
   Is the issue inside HR, across business functions, across ecosystems, or across human-machine work?
   问题是在 HR 内部、跨业务职能、跨生态，还是跨人机协作？

3. Operating model and organization design / 运营模式与组织设计
   What structure, roles, networks of teams, KPIs, and governance translate strategy into execution?
   什么结构、角色、团队网络、KPI 和治理能把战略转化为执行？

4. Workforce insight and technology / 劳动力洞察与技术
   What data, analytics, skills, platforms, and AI/workforce technology change the decision quality?
   哪些数据、分析、技能、平台和 AI/劳动力技术能提升决策质量？

5. Experience, rewards, and performance / 体验、回报与绩效
   How do performance, rewards, culture, and experience reinforce the desired work outcomes?
   绩效、回报、文化和体验如何共同强化目标工作结果？

6. Change and adoption / 变革与采纳
   How will leaders, managers, HR, employees, and external stakeholders adopt the new way of working?
   领导者、管理者、HR、员工和外部利益相关方如何采纳新的工作方式？
```

## Method Families / 方法族

### 1. Human Performance and Human Outcomes / 人类绩效与人的结果

Use this when leaders ask how HR contributes to enterprise value. Deloitte's recent Human Capital Trends materials frame performance through both business outcomes and human outcomes. The practical move is to stop treating wellbeing, skills, trust, belonging, work design, and productivity as separate topics.  
当管理层追问 HR 如何贡献企业价值时使用。Deloitte 近年 Human Capital Trends 将绩效放在“业务结果 + 人的结果”的共同框架中。实操关键是：不要再把幸福感、技能、信任、归属感、工作设计和生产力拆成彼此无关的问题。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which business outcome is constrained by workforce behavior, capability, or experience? / 哪个业务结果被劳动力行为、能力或体验限制？
- Which human outcomes are leading indicators of performance? / 哪些人的结果是绩效的前置信号？
- Are leaders optimizing short-term productivity while damaging trust, skills, or adaptability? / 领导者是否在优化短期生产力，却损害信任、技能或适应力？
- What would improve both business value and human value? / 什么动作能同时改善业务价值和人的价值？

Tool / 工具：

```text
Business outcome -> Human outcome -> Work design -> Workforce capability -> Metrics -> Adoption
业务结果 -> 人的结果 -> 工作设计 -> 劳动力能力 -> 指标 -> 采纳
```

### 2. Boundaryless HR / 无边界 HR

Use this when HR is too siloed to solve workforce problems. Deloitte's 2024 boundaryless HR theme argues that HR should evolve from a specialized function into a discipline integrated with people, business, and community. In practice, people expertise becomes shared by HR, line leaders, technology teams, finance, operations, and employees.  
当 HR 过于孤立，无法解决复杂劳动力问题时使用。Deloitte 2024 年“boundaryless HR”主题认为，HR 应从专业职能进化为与员工、业务和社区融合的纪律/能力。实操中，人的专业能力不再只属于 HR，而是由 HR、一线领导、技术团队、财务、运营和员工共同承担。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which workforce decisions are trapped inside HR instead of owned with the business? / 哪些劳动力决策被困在 HR 内部，而没有和业务共同拥有？
- Which people problems require finance, operations, technology, legal, or external ecosystem input? / 哪些人的问题需要财务、运营、技术、法务或外部生态参与？
- What people capabilities should every manager own? / 哪些人的能力应该由每个管理者承担？
- Where should HR stop being a service provider and become a discipline steward? / HR 应在哪些地方停止只做服务提供者，转为纪律守护者？

Tool / 工具：

```text
HR-owned activity
-> Business-owned workforce decision
-> Shared people discipline
-> Data/technology support
-> Governance and accountability
HR 独有活动
-> 业务共同拥有的劳动力决策
-> 共享的人才/人员纪律
-> 数据与技术支持
-> 治理与问责
```

### 3. High-Impact HR Operating Model / 高影响力 HR 运营模式

Use this when HR transformation is stuck at shared services, technology implementation, or org-chart redesign. Deloitte's High-Impact HR materials emphasize starting with the HR customer, evolving HR capabilities, digitizing the workplace, owning workforce data, and getting beyond structure.  
当 HR 转型停留在共享服务、系统上线或组织架构图时使用。Deloitte 的 High-Impact HR 材料强调从 HR 客户出发、升级 HR 能力、数字化工作场景、拥有劳动力数据，并超越结构本身。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Who is the HR customer in this decision: employee, manager, executive, candidate, or workforce segment? / 这个决策里的 HR 客户是谁：员工、管理者、高管、候选人还是某类劳动力群体？
- Which HR capabilities must change: consulting, analytics, digital, adaptability, agility, product ownership, service delivery? / 哪些 HR 能力必须改变：咨询、分析、数字化、适应性、敏捷、产品负责、服务交付？
- Is HR technology embedded in the work experience or bolted onto old processes? / HR 技术是嵌入工作体验，还是贴在旧流程外面？
- Does HR own meaningful workforce data and turn it into business insight? / HR 是否拥有有意义的劳动力数据，并把它转化为业务洞察？

Tool / 工具：

```text
Customer need -> HR capability -> Digital workflow -> Workforce data -> Insight -> Business decision
客户需求 -> HR 能力 -> 数字化流程 -> 劳动力数据 -> 洞察 -> 业务决策
```

### 4. Organization Design and Networked Teams / 组织设计与网络化团队

Use this when strategy is not translating into execution. Deloitte's organization design service pages emphasize translating business strategy into structure, roles and responsibilities, work, worker and networks of teams, KPIs, and governance.  
当战略无法转化为执行时使用。Deloitte 组织设计服务页面强调：将业务战略翻译为结构、角色责任、工作、员工和团队网络，并配套 KPI 与治理。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- What strategy is the current organization failing to execute? / 当前组织无法执行什么战略？
- Are roles and decision rights clear enough for speed? / 角色和决策权是否足够清晰，能支撑速度？
- Which work should be done by stable teams, agile squads, communities, platforms, or external partners? / 哪些工作应由稳定团队、敏捷小队、社群、平台或外部伙伴完成？
- Which KPIs and governance forums reinforce the desired team network? / 哪些 KPI 和治理会议能强化目标团队网络？

Tool / 工具：

```text
Strategy -> Work outcomes -> Work architecture -> Roles/responsibilities -> Team network -> KPIs -> Governance
战略 -> 工作结果 -> 工作架构 -> 角色责任 -> 团队网络 -> KPI -> 治理
```

### 5. Performance Management Reinvention / 绩效管理重塑

Use this when performance management is process-heavy but performance-light. Deloitte's performance management materials caution that process optimization alone does not unlock human performance. The design question is how performance conversations, goals, teams, skills, rewards, and manager behavior reinforce real work outcomes.  
当绩效管理流程很重但绩效改善很弱时使用。Deloitte 绩效管理资料提醒：单纯优化流程无法释放人类绩效。真正的问题是：绩效对话、目标、团队、技能、回报和管理者行为如何共同强化真实工作结果。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Is the performance process measuring work or improving work? / 绩效流程是在衡量工作，还是改善工作？
- Do goals reflect individual work, team work, network contribution, and human outcomes? / 目标是否反映个人工作、团队工作、网络贡献和人的结果？
- Are managers equipped for continuous performance conversations? / 管理者是否具备持续绩效对话能力？
- How do rewards and recognition reinforce the new performance logic? / 回报和认可如何强化新的绩效逻辑？

### 6. Total Rewards and Workforce Experience / 全面回报与劳动力体验

Use this when compensation, benefits, recognition, wellbeing, career, and experience are fragmented. Deloitte's rewards materials point toward technology-enabled, experience-aware, and strategy-linked rewards.  
当薪酬、福利、认可、健康、职业发展和体验彼此割裂时使用。Deloitte 全面回报资料指向技术赋能、体验导向和战略挂钩的回报体系。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which rewards matter to which workforce segments? / 哪类劳动力群体真正看重哪些回报？
- Are rewards differentiated by role, skills, performance, scarcity, and experience needs? / 回报是否按岗位、技能、绩效、稀缺性和体验需求差异化？
- Is rewards technology improving understanding and choice? / 回报技术是否提升员工理解和选择？
- Where are rewards costs high but perceived value low? / 哪些回报成本高但员工感知价值低？

### 7. Human-Machine Workforce and AI / 人机协作与 AI 劳动力

Use this when AI changes work allocation, skills, governance, or workforce planning. Deloitte public materials increasingly address human and machine work. The HR implication is not just reskilling; it is redesigning work, accountability, data governance, and the human advantage.  
当 AI 改变工作分配、技能、治理或劳动力规划时使用。Deloitte 公开资料越来越多讨论人与机器的工作。对应的 HR 含义不只是再培训，而是重新设计工作、问责、数据治理和人的优势。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which tasks should be automated, augmented, or protected for human judgment? / 哪些任务应自动化、增强化，哪些必须保留人的判断？
- What skills become more valuable when AI enters the workflow? / AI 进入流程后，哪些技能更有价值？
- Who is accountable for AI-assisted decisions? / AI 辅助决策由谁负责？
- How will the organization preserve trust, fairness, privacy, and explainability? / 组织如何保护信任、公平、隐私和可解释性？

## Anti-Patterns / 反模式

- Treating Human Capital Trends as buzzwords rather than decision tools.  
  把 Human Capital Trends 当成流行词，而不是决策工具。
- Running HR transformation as an HR technology implementation only.  
  把 HR 转型仅仅做成 HR 系统上线。
- Designing HR operating models around HR structure instead of HR customers and workforce decisions.  
  围绕 HR 组织架构，而不是 HR 客户和劳动力决策来设计 HR 运营模式。
- Optimizing performance management process without changing manager behavior and work outcomes.  
  优化绩效流程，却不改变管理者行为和工作结果。
- Using AI to automate old work without redesigning accountability, skills, trust, and governance.  
  用 AI 自动化旧工作，却不重设问责、技能、信任和治理。
