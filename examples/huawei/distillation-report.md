## Core Distillation / 核心蒸馏

The final Skill is not a Huawei profile. It converts public evidence into a practical methodology around seven method families.  
最终 Skill 不是华为公司介绍，而是把公开证据转化为七类可调用方法。

1. Strategy-led organization design / 战略牵引的组织设计
2. Digital talent ecosystem / 数字人才生态
3. R&D and expert talent density / 研发与专家人才密度
4. Contribution-based performance and incentives / 基于贡献的绩效与激励
5. Cadre and leadership engine / 干部与领导力引擎
6. Organizational resilience / 组织韧性
7. Global-local talent management / 全球本地化人才管理

## Best Use / 最适合用途

- Diagnose organization design problems in technology, complex delivery, or ecosystem businesses.  
  诊断技术型、复杂交付型或生态型业务的组织设计问题。
- Design talent strategy for R&D, expert roles, leadership pipelines, and digital capability.  
  设计研发、专家岗位、干部梯队和数字能力相关的人才战略。
- Redesign performance and incentives around contribution, responsibility, and customer/business impact.  
  围绕贡献、责任和客户/业务影响重设计绩效与激励。
- Build organization resilience and global-local talent operating rules.  
  构建组织韧性和全球本地化人才运营规则。

## Boundary / 边界

Do not present this Skill as Huawei official policy or internal HR doctrine. Detailed pay formulas, cadre assessment rules, confidential organizational structures, or private training content are outside the evidence base.  
不要把本 Skill 表述为华为官方政策或内部 HR 教义。具体薪酬公式、干部评价规则、保密组织结构和内部培训内容都不在证据范围内。
