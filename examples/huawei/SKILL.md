## Core Huawei-Style Logic / 华为风格核心逻辑

Use this six-layer logic before designing any HR or organization solution.  
设计任何 HR 或组织方案前，先使用六层逻辑。

```text
1. Business battlefield / 业务战场
   Which customer, technology, market, or delivery battle must the organization win?
   组织必须赢下哪个客户、技术、市场或交付战场？

2. Value-creating roles / 价值创造岗位
   Which roles and teams create the most strategic value or constrain execution?
   哪些岗位和团队创造最大战略价值，或正在限制执行？

3. Capability density / 能力密度
   What expertise, learning speed, digital capability, and leadership capacity are required?
   需要什么专业能力、学习速度、数字能力和领导力容量？

4. Contribution mechanism / 贡献机制
   How are responsibility, performance, incentives, and recognition linked to value contribution?
   责任、绩效、激励和认可如何与价值贡献挂钩？

5. Organizational resilience / 组织韧性
   Can the organization absorb shocks, reallocate resources, preserve learning, and keep serving customers?
   组织能否承受冲击、重新配置资源、沉淀学习并持续服务客户？

6. Ecosystem leverage / 生态杠杆
   Which universities, partners, developers, customers, and regional ecosystems must be developed with the firm?
   哪些高校、伙伴、开发者、客户和区域生态需要与企业共同发展？
```

## Method Families / 方法族

### 1. Strategy-Led Organization Design / 战略牵引的组织设计

Use this when the problem is not a generic HR issue but a mismatch between strategy and organizational capacity. Huawei public materials emphasize technology investment, global operations, digital infrastructure, and serving customers across many regions. In HR diagnosis, start from the business battlefield rather than from HR process defects.  
当问题不是普通 HR 问题，而是战略与组织能力不匹配时使用。华为公开资料强调技术投入、全球运营、数字基础设施和跨区域客户服务。做 HR 诊断时，应从业务战场出发，而不是从 HR 流程缺陷出发。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which strategic battle requires a different organization shape? / 哪个战略战场需要不同的组织形态？
- Which decision rights are too slow for customer or technology cycles? / 哪些决策权跟不上客户或技术周期？
- Which teams must be closer to customers, R&D, delivery, or ecosystem partners? / 哪些团队必须更贴近客户、研发、交付或生态伙伴？
- What work should be centralized for scale, and what should be localized for speed? / 哪些工作应集中化以获得规模，哪些应本地化以获得速度？

Tool / 工具：

```text
Strategy battle -> Critical capabilities -> Critical roles -> Decision rights -> Metrics -> Talent actions
战略战场 -> 关键能力 -> 关键岗位 -> 决策权 -> 指标 -> 人才动作
```

### 2. Digital Talent Ecosystem / 数字人才生态

Use this when a company needs digital capability beyond its own payroll. Huawei official public pages around talent initiatives, talent summits, education programs, and ICT competitions point to an ecosystem approach: develop students, partners, developers, customers, and regional talent pools.  
当企业需要的数字能力超出自身员工边界时使用。华为围绕人才峰会、数字人才倡议、教育项目和 ICT 竞赛的公开资料，体现的是生态型人才建设：培养学生、伙伴、开发者、客户和区域人才池。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Is the capability gap internal only, or ecosystem-wide? / 能力缺口只在公司内部，还是存在于整个生态？
- Which universities, partners, and customer organizations must learn with us? / 哪些高校、伙伴和客户组织需要与我们共同学习？
- What certifications, competitions, labs, academies, or community programs can scale learning? / 哪些认证、竞赛、实验室、学院或社区项目能放大学习？
- How will ecosystem talent translate into sales, delivery, innovation, and adoption? / 生态人才如何转化为销售、交付、创新和采纳？

Tool / 工具：

```text
Capability gap
-> Internal talent build
-> Partner enablement
-> University pipeline
-> Customer capability building
-> Certification/community flywheel
能力缺口
-> 内部人才建设
-> 伙伴赋能
-> 高校人才管道
-> 客户能力建设
-> 认证/社区飞轮
```

### 3. R&D and Expert Talent Density / 研发与专家人才密度

Use this when the organization competes through technology depth, product reliability, or complex delivery. Huawei public annual reports and official narratives highlight large-scale R&D investment and technical innovation. The HR implication is that talent strategy must protect expert depth, learning speed, and technical continuity.  
当组织依靠技术深度、产品可靠性或复杂交付竞争时使用。华为公开年报和官方叙事突出大规模研发投入与技术创新。对应的 HR 含义是：人才战略必须保护专家深度、学习速度和技术连续性。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which technical domains are strategically non-negotiable? / 哪些技术领域是战略上不可退让的？
- Are expert career paths as attractive as management paths? / 专家通道是否与管理通道同样有吸引力？
- Where is knowledge locked in individuals rather than teams or platforms? / 哪些知识被锁在个人身上，而不是团队或平台里？
- Which roles need long-cycle accumulation rather than short-cycle rotation? / 哪些岗位需要长期积累，而不是短期轮岗？

Management actions / 管理动作：

- Build dual career ladders for experts and managers. / 建立专家与管理双通道。
- Protect time for deep technical learning and problem solving. / 保护深度技术学习和攻关时间。
- Link talent review to strategic capability maps, not only current performance. / 人才盘点挂钩战略能力地图，而不只看当前绩效。

### 4. Contribution-Based Performance and Incentives / 基于贡献的绩效与激励

Use this when the organization needs stronger accountability and clearer value contribution. Public and third-party Huawei discussions often describe performance management around responsibility, contribution, customer value, and results. Treat detailed pay formulas as unverified unless official evidence is provided.  
当组织需要更强问责和更清晰的价值贡献时使用。公开及第三方华为研究常围绕责任、贡献、客户价值和结果讨论绩效管理。除非有官方证据，不要把具体薪酬公式当作事实。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- What is the value contribution of each role or team? / 每个岗位或团队的价值贡献是什么？
- Are KPIs measuring activity, output, customer impact, or strategic contribution? / KPI 衡量的是动作、产出、客户影响，还是战略贡献？
- Does high contribution receive differentiated recognition? / 高贡献是否得到差异化认可？
- Where are incentives encouraging local optimization instead of enterprise value? / 哪些激励在鼓励局部最优，而不是企业价值？

Tool / 工具：

```text
Role mission -> Responsibility result -> Customer/business impact -> Contribution evidence -> Differentiated reward
岗位使命 -> 责任结果 -> 客户/业务影响 -> 贡献证据 -> 差异化激励
```

### 5. Cadre and Leadership Engine / 干部与领导力引擎

Use this when leadership bottlenecks slow execution. For a Huawei-inspired benchmark, leadership is not only a competency model; it is an execution engine that connects strategy, accountability, customer orientation, team development, and resilience under pressure.  
当领导力瓶颈拖慢执行时使用。在华为启发式标杆中，领导力不只是能力模型，而是连接战略、问责、客户导向、团队发展和压力下韧性的执行引擎。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which leaders can convert strategy into operating rhythm? / 哪些领导者能把战略转化为运营节奏？
- Which leaders build successors and expert teams, not just personal results? / 哪些领导者培养接班人与专家团队，而不只是个人业绩？
- Are leadership standards tied to customer value and team capability? / 领导力标准是否与客户价值和团队能力挂钩？
- Where do managers avoid difficult accountability conversations? / 哪些管理者回避困难的问责沟通？

Output template / 输出模板：

```markdown
## Leadership Bottleneck Map / 领导力瓶颈图
- Strategic translation / 战略翻译
- Decision speed / 决策速度
- Talent development / 人才发展
- Cross-boundary collaboration / 跨边界协同
- Pressure resilience / 压力韧性
- Customer orientation / 客户导向
```

### 6. Organizational Resilience / 组织韧性

Use this when a company faces shocks, geopolitical pressure, supply disruption, technology uncertainty, or rapid market shifts. Huawei public material includes resilience-oriented narratives. Translate this into HR terms: redundant capability, fast redeployment, knowledge continuity, morale under pressure, and disciplined execution.  
当企业面对冲击、地缘压力、供应中断、技术不确定或市场快速变化时使用。华为公开资料中有组织韧性相关叙事。转化为 HR 语言就是：能力冗余、快速调配、知识连续性、压力下士气和纪律性执行。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which capabilities must not have a single point of failure? / 哪些能力不能存在单点故障？
- Can talent be redeployed fast without destroying accountability? / 人才能否快速调配，同时不破坏问责？
- Where is institutional memory weak? / 哪些地方组织记忆薄弱？
- How does the organization maintain motivation during prolonged pressure? / 长期压力下组织如何保持动机？

### 7. Global-Local Talent Management / 全球本地化人才管理

Use this when a company operates across countries, cultures, labor markets, and customer contexts. Huawei public sources include global footprint and regional talent initiatives. The key design issue is balancing global standards with local legitimacy.  
当企业跨国家、文化、劳动力市场和客户场景运营时使用。华为公开资料包含全球业务覆盖和区域人才倡议。关键设计问题是平衡全球标准与本地合法性/合理性。

Diagnostic questions / 诊断问题：

- Which standards must remain global? / 哪些标准必须全球一致？
- Which practices must adapt to local labor law, culture, and market expectations? / 哪些实践必须适配本地劳动法、文化和市场预期？
- Are local leaders empowered enough to serve customers? / 本地领导者是否有足够授权服务客户？
- Where does headquarters control slow local execution? / 总部控制在哪些地方拖慢本地执行？

## Confidence Labels / 信心标签

- `High confidence / 高信心`: Supported by official Huawei public sources and repeated third-party sources.  
  有华为官方公开资料支持，且被多个第三方来源重复印证。
- `Medium confidence / 中信心`: Supported by several third-party sources or one official directional source.  
  由多个第三方来源支持，或由一个官方方向性来源支持。
- `Low confidence / 低信心`: Practitioner interpretation or single-source claim.  
  从业者解读或单一来源主张。
- `Needs verification / 需要验证`: Current, confidential, numeric, legal, compensation, or region-specific claim.  
  涉及最新、保密、数字、法律、薪酬或地区差异的主张。

## Anti-Patterns / 反模式

- Copying Huawei's pressure, slogans, or surface management rituals without Huawei's business context.  
  脱离华为业务语境，照搬压力、口号或表层管理仪式。
- Treating third-party descriptions of Huawei HR as official internal policy.  
  把第三方对华为 HR 的描述当作官方内部政策。
- Designing contribution-based performance without clear role missions and calibration governance.  
  没有岗位使命和校准治理，就设计贡献型绩效。
- Building digital talent programs that do not connect to customer adoption, delivery, or ecosystem outcomes.  
  数字人才项目没有连接客户采纳、交付或生态结果。
- Over-centralizing global standards and killing local execution speed.  
  过度集中全球标准，牺牲本地执行速度。
