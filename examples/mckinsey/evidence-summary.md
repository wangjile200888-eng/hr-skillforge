# McKinsey Evidence Summary
# 麦肯锡证据摘要

## Strongest Evidence / 最强证据

- Official McKinsey People & Organizational Performance pages support a people/organization lens tied to sustained performance.  
  麦肯锡 People & Organizational Performance 官方页面支持“人才与组织连接持续绩效”的总视角。
- Official Talent, Leadership, People Analytics, Change Management, Organization Design, and Organize to Value pages support the main solution domains.  
  官方 Talent、Leadership、People Analytics、Change Management、Organization Design 和 Organize to Value 页面支持主要解决方案领域。
- McKinsey reports such as HR Monitor 2025, State of Organizations, and Performance through people support the broader HR, organization, and human-capital logic.  
  HR Monitor 2025、State of Organizations、Performance through people 等麦肯锡报告支持更广泛的 HR、组织和人力资本逻辑。
- Performance management public articles support design around goals, reviews, development, rewards, and manager capability.  
  绩效管理公开文章支持围绕目标、评估、发展、激励和管理者能力进行系统设计。
- McKinsey Talks Talent and related podcast pages support a video/audio evidence lane, but transcript text is not yet strong.  
  McKinsey Talks Talent 及相关播客页面支持视频/音频证据通道，但 transcript 正文仍不足。

## Evidence Gaps / 证据缺口

- Downloaded subtitles / 已下载字幕: 0
- Clean transcript files / 已清洗 transcript 文件: 0
- Some Chinese source cards show encoding noise in fetched raw text; use official URLs and clean summaries when localizing.  
  部分中文 source card 的抓取原文存在编码噪声；做中国本土化时应优先使用官方 URL 和清洗后的摘要。
- PDF text extraction is still lightweight; deeper PDF extraction would improve model richness.  
  PDF 正文抽取仍较轻量；进一步抽取 PDF 正文会提升方法论丰富度。

## Evidence Use Rule / 证据使用规则

Use official pages and reports for primary claims. Use podcast/video pages for directional themes only unless transcript text is provided. Use third-party and external sources for boundary checks, not as primary McKinsey authority.  
主要判断应优先使用官方页面和报告。除非提供 transcript 正文，播客/视频页面只作为方向性主题证据。第三方和外部来源用于边界检查，不作为麦肯锡主要权威来源。
