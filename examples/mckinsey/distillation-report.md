## Core Models Distilled / 蒸馏出的核心模型

1. Value-at-stake first: HR work should start from the business value constrained by people or organization.  
   价值优先：HR 工作应从被人才或组织限制的业务价值开始。
2. Talent-to-value: identify roles and skill pools that disproportionately create or protect value.  
   人才到价值：识别对价值创造或价值保护影响不成比例的岗位与技能池。
3. Organization design / Organize to Value: align strategy, decision rights, operating-model fingerprint, accountabilities, and culture.  
   组织设计 / 围绕价值组织：对齐战略、决策权、运营模式指纹、责任和文化。
4. Performance management: connect goal setting, feedback, differentiation, rewards, development, and manager capability.  
   绩效管理：连接目标、反馈、区分度、激励、发展和管理者能力。
5. Leadership and organizational health: develop leadership behaviors and routines tied to strategy and transformation.  
   领导力与组织健康：发展与战略和转型绑定的领导行为与管理例行机制。
6. Change management: make behavior shifts, adoption metrics, reinforcement, and capability building explicit.  
   变革管理：明确行为转变、采纳指标、强化机制和能力建设。
7. People analytics: start from a business decision, not a dashboard.  
   People Analytics：从业务决策开始，而不是从仪表盘开始。
8. HR operating model: segment strategic, advisory, product, analytics, service, and automated HR work.  
   HR 运营模式：区分战略、顾问、产品、分析、服务和自动化 HR 工作。

## Quality Boundary / 质量边界

The source-count, official-source, recent-source, and video/audio-candidate gates reach Full Version. However, subtitle/transcript text remains thin because browser cookie extraction for yt-dlp failed and platform access was limited. The Skill therefore treats video/podcast sources as candidate directional evidence unless an authorized transcript is provided.  
来源数、官方来源、近三年来源和视频/音频候选来源均达到 Full Version 数量门槛。但由于 yt-dlp 浏览器 Cookie 抽取失败且平台访问受限，字幕/transcript 正文仍然薄。因此，除非后续提供授权 transcript，本 Skill 将视频/播客来源视为候选方向性证据。
