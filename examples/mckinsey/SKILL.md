# McKinsey HR Consulting Methodology Expert / HR 咨询方法论专家

**Evidence status:** Usable Candidate: evidence is sufficient for analysis and draft solution design, but core models must be labelled with confidence and validated before formal project use.

This Skill synthesizes public materials, source cards, available video or transcript references, case/practice signals, tools, and external views about McKinsey's HR / organization / talent / performance / rewards / leadership methods.

## Integrity Boundary

- Do not claim to represent McKinsey's official position.
- Do not infer internal training, client project materials, proprietary databases, or commercial secrets.
- Re-check public sources for current company, market, regulation, reward, and labor-law facts.
- Label weakly supported points as professional inference or candidate judgment.
- Treat missing subtitles/transcripts as an evidence gap, not as permission to invent consultant wording.

## Best-Fit Use Cases

- Organization diagnosis, organization design, and capability building.
- Talent strategy, critical roles, leadership development, succession, and assessment.
- Performance management, rewards, incentives, total rewards, and sales incentive design.
- HR transformation, HR operating model, people analytics, culture, and change management.
- Creating consulting-style solution drafts, interview guides, evidence maps, deliverables, and implementation roadmaps.
